Manuscript of the Graviton Flow Theory (GFT)

as proposal to unify the essential characteristics of Quantum Mechanics and General Theory of Relativity respectively the Standard Model of Particle Physics and the Standard Model of Cosmology with a non-classical, fractal and self organized particle system

The Graviton Flow Theory is a philosophical based and axiomatic framed ansatz to realize the Theory of Everything (TOE) searched in contemporary physics. Below you find the actual abstract and summary. The actual version in german language is downloadable as PDF. For a discussion or critique please have contact with me.


Abstract

The Graviton Flow Theory is a new formulation of the Quantum Mechanics that comes out of a philosophical and fundamental principle of how a three-dimensional elementary particle is embeded in space-time. This principle is analysed and developed further by yet known observations and simple proposals about the space-time of a observer and the space-time of the cosmos. The modelation of the new theory leads to a world where the structure of the space-time is constituted of fundamental unified particles which composing structure particles close to be photons, but only certain of them are. The most of them are non-polarized photons and therefore not directly observable. The nature falls apart in two realms. On one hand the directly observable realm based on photons and the other known elementary particles. And the other hand the not directly observable realm of the vacuum space-time, consisting of a big amount of new non-polarized photons which transmit all the forces, sometimes together with their polarized cousins. The space-time system of both realms gives the chance to unify the forces — including gravity — and create the quantum effects we do see in experiments. The arising gravitational potential differs from the Newtonian one and from that of the General Relativity at small acceleration and has the possibility to explain gravity in and between galaxies and the Pioneer Effect. A fractal enhanced Graviton Flow Theory unifies the genesis of black holes in a cosmos and a big bang at a lower level of space-time structure. This model gives possibility to answer cosmological observations like the cosmological constant as well as the flat cosmos space-time and may offer solutions for several related paradoxi.


Summary

The Graviton Flow Theory works on a basis of assumtions about the characteristics of embedding the elementary particles into a space-time structure. It turns out that from this point of view something fit not really good togehter in the Standard model of Particle Physics. This concerns how time is established in the quantum theories. Distinguishing between the terms aging and time — which had the same meaning until today — eliminates the problem. Out of the basic assumtions, conclusions and completive postulates establishes a axiomatic system of physical principles. Their physical realisation leads to a model that grounds on a self organized particle system that expands the term of light to the existence of a non-polarized photon. In the new model time is a property of the cosmos and is equivalent to the constant velocity of new established fundamental particles of space-time. The aging appears as a particular component of the time velocity which is equivalent to the velocity of light as well as to the structural aging process of the elementary particles. The elementary particles appears as clocks of light. An extension of the term of light to the whole space-time structure shows a mechanism of gravitational intermediation that is based on a structure of density and movement of the fundamental particles. Therefore huge amounts of the new non-polarized photon composed of the fundamental particles constitutes the curvature of space-time — if polarized appears as photon. The Graviton Flow Theory describes a qualitative structure of a photon and by doing that the structure of the elementary particles and their interactions. This leads to the source of gravitation and leads as well to a new negativ pole of it. All forces seem to be unified and are based on gravitation. Bosons and Fermions are unified, because Bosons are structural parts of the Fermions. All particles are inseparable interweaved with each other by the steady flow of the fundamental particles and exists because of their stored information and because of their steady information flow. The essential characteristics of the Quantum Mechanics and the Theory of General Relativity seems to be unified. The Graviton Flow Theory is enhanced as a fractal structure of the expansion of the term of light by launching the presumption that the observer horizont of the cosmos is the same boundary surface as the observer horizont of a black hole, just from two different points of view. This leads to the conclusion that the univers is build of cascaded cosmoi. The model showes an ansatz to explain cosmology observations that are handled by the Standard Model of Cosmology or which are today subjects of actual hypothesis in cosmology.
